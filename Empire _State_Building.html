The Empire State Building

The Empire State building is one of the most remarkable buildings of New York City. It is located in the Fifth Avenue between 33rd and 34th Streets. According to its official site, the Empire State Building was inaugurated on May 1st, 1931 by President Hoover. This building is one of the most visited places in New York not only by tourist but by Americans. In 2014, during my vacations, I came with my family to visit this building. At that time, I saw the Empire State as a great place where you can see all New York and take amazing pictures of it. However, this year, during my visit, once I know all the history of New York City and its evolution, I realized the importance it has. The Empire State Building is a 102-story Art Deco skyscraper in the Midtown South neighborhood of Manhattan in New York City. The building was designed by Shreve, Lamb & Harmon and built from 1930 to 1931. Its name is derived from "Empire State", the nickname of the state of New York.
